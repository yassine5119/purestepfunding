Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers about challenges, payouts, trading rules and funded accounts with PureStep Funding.

How fast are payouts?

Payouts are processed within 1 hour to 24 hours after review approval.

What platform do you support?

PureStep Funding currently supports MetaTrader 5 (MT5).

Do you offer crypto payouts?

Yes. Traders can receive payouts using Binance Pay and USDT.

What is the profit split?

Traders can receive up to 100% profit split depending on account type.

How long does account review take?

Challenge reviews are completed within 1 hour to 24 hours.

Can I trade during news events?

Yes, trading during news is allowed unless otherwise specified in account rules.

What happens if I violate the rules?

Accounts that violate trading rules may be breached automatically.

Do you allow Expert Advisors (EAs)?

Yes, EAs are allowed if they comply with our policies.

How do I become funded?

Choose a challenge, pass the evaluation and receive a funded trading account.

Do accounts expire?

No. Accounts remain active while respecting trading rules.

Can I hold trades overnight?

Yes, overnight and weekend holding is available on selected account types.

How can I contact support?

You can contact our support team through Instagram or dashboard support.

Still Need Help?

Our support team is ready to help you with your funded trading journey.
